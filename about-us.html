About Us

First Ever League of Bangladeshi Digital Superheroes for One-Stop Solution Since 2008

Whether you are a big shot in the world of fast moving consumer goods, or you are a game changer in the world of financial services, development sector, smartphone, or even banking, nutrition, superstores or online marketplace, there is one key partner that you will hear the name of Analyzen: The first ever one stop Digital Agency of Bangladesh with one of the largest portfolios of brands. No matter what your requirement, be it overall Strategy, Digital-first 360 Content Development, Enterprise Software, Web Experience, Apps and Games development, Analytics, Community Management, or Digital Media Buying, unlike most other agencies of the country, we have every support under one roof!

The Beginning

Analyzen, a dream that started as a software development company in the University dormitories in 2008 by two young graduating students Sumit Saha and Ridwan Hafiz from BUET Computer Science & Engineering, has now turned into a successful Digital Agency, capable of full digital and creative support, catering to both local and international organizations. While Sumit Saha has been the Technology Expert of the Company since its inception to drive innovations and harness power of technology to solve real consumer problems, Ridwan Hafiz has always been the Creative Expert who envisioned the growing digital market of the country and wanted to explore this potential opportunity. The team benefitted from a Brand and Strategic Planning perspective, when Risalat Siddique a seasoned Brand Professional with multi-country experience joined as a co-investor for the company in 2014. The company was converted into a limited company in early 2015.
